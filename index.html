specialties

Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a refreshing cold soup made from ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, and garlic, blended with olive oil and vinegar.

Originating from Spain, it’s perfect for hot summer days. Light, flavorful, and healthy — a true Mediterranean classic.

Seafood pasta

Seafood pasta is a delicious dish made with tender pasta and a mix of fresh seafood like shrimp, mussels, and squid.

It's cooked in a rich garlic, white wine, and tomato sauce. Full of flavor and elegance, it brings the taste of the sea to your table.

Lemon Meringue Pie

Lemon Meringue Pie is a classic dessert with a zesty lemon filling and a fluffy, golden meringue topping.

Set in a buttery, flaky crust, it offers the perfect balance of sweet and tart. Light and refreshing, it's a delightful way to end any meal.
